Government Internship & Course Portals

Free Internship and Certificate Course Platforms by Government of India

Internship & Job Portals

AICTE Internship Portal

Website : https://internship.aicte-india.org/

  • Types of Work: Government-approved internships in engineering, tech, and management. Both online and offline work formats available. Projects related to coding, marketing, and research.

  • Eligibility: Graduate/Postgraduate students. Good academic performance (75%+ or 7.5+ CGPA).

  • Special Features: 3-step easy registration. Certificate after completion. 30,000+ internships updated every month.

NITI Aayog Internship Portal

Website : https://www.niti.gov.in/internship

  • Types of Work: Research and report writing. Government policies and planning projects work. Economics and public administration related tasks.

  • Eligibility: Graduate/Postgraduate students. Good academic performance (75%+ or 7.5+ CGPA).

  • Special Features: Summer & Winter internship cycles. Apply with Aadhaar + College ID. High prestige & strong resume impact.

MyGov Internship Portal

Website : https://www.mygov.in/

  • Types of Work: Content writing and poster design. Social media campaigns. Government awareness projects work.

  • Eligibility: 10th/12th pass, graduate students. Writing or communication skills helpful.

  • Special Features: Mostly online work. Government of India certificate. Chance to work on real schemes.

MoE (Ministry of Education) Internship Platform

Website : Listed under AICTE or MyGov

  • Types of Work: Education projects like curriculum improvement. Research & content creation. Teaching-related awareness programs.

  • Eligibility: College students interested in education field. Communication skills helpful.

  • Special Features: Work with education ministry. Best for teaching/research aspirants.

NSDC Portal (National Skill Development Corporation)

Website : https://nsdcindia.org/

  • Types of Work: Skill-based internships (healthcare, retail, IT, etc.). On-the-job training projects. Industry-specific tasks.

  • Eligibility: 10th/12th pass. Field-related basic knowledge.

  • Special Features: NSDC certification. Job placement help. Both Internship + Training.

Skill India Portal

Website : https://admin.skillindiadigital.gov.in/login

  • Types of Work: Vocational projects like electrician, data entry, beauty work. Hands-on training with real tools. Freelancing or job-ready work.

  • Eligibility: 10th, 12th pass, or college student. No high degree needed.

  • Special Features: India-wide valid certificates. Local language support. Best for starting a career.

Certificate Course Platforms

SWAYAM (MHRD Govt Platform)

Website : https://swayam.gov.in/

  • What is it? It is an online learning website made by the Indian government for students of schools, colleges, and job seekers.

  • Courses Available: School-level (9th to 12th), College subjects (BA, BCom, BSc, BTech), Soft skills & personality development, Language learning, law, business, computer courses.

  • Duration: 4 weeks to 12 weeks (1 to 3 months)

  • Eligibility: Anyone from class 9 onwards. No age limit or fees to learn. Exams are optional (for certificate).

  • Special Features: Taught by college professors (IIT, DU, etc.). Free learning videos and notes. You get a certificate if you pass the final exam (optional & paid).

NPTEL (by IITs)

Website : https://nptel.ac.in/

  • What is it? This is a top-level learning portal by IITs for technical and engineering students.

  • Courses Available: Computer science, electronics, civil, mechanical, Artificial Intelligence, Python, data science, Engineering mathematics, physics, etc.

  • Duration: 4, 8, or 12 weeks

  • Eligibility: Anyone from class 11 onward (if interested in engineering/tech). Best for B.Tech, BSc, and diploma students.

  • Special Features: Courses by IIT professors. Free to learn, but ₹1000 for exam + certificate. Government-recognized certificate.

AICTE ELIS Portal

Website : https://free.aicte-india.org/

  • What is it? A free e-learning portal by the AICTE for engineering and management students.

  • Courses Available: Programming (Java, Python, C++), Cybersecurity, data science, machine learning, Business & management skills.

  • Duration: 4 to 10 weeks (mostly short-term)

  • Eligibility: Students of diploma, BBA, B.Tech, etc. Also suitable for 12th-pass students with interest.

  • Special Features: All courses are free. Certificates after completion. Partnership with companies like IBM, Microsoft.

NSDC (National Skill Development Corporation)

Website : https://nsdcindia.org/

  • What is it? It offers training and courses for job-ready skills in various industries.

  • Courses Available: Retail, healthcare, IT, banking, Electrician, beautician, digital marketing, Plumber, mechanic, customer service.

  • Duration: 2 weeks to 6 months (depends on course)

  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass. No high qualification needed.

  • Special Features: Skill development + certification. Helps in job placement. Available in many local languages.

Skill India Digital

Website : https://www.skillindiadigital.gov.in/home

  • What is it? Govt platform to learn job-ready digital skills.

  • Courses Available: Computer basics, digital marketing, Data entry, office assistant, Web designing, coding, AI basics.

  • Duration: 1 week to 3 months

  • Eligibility: Anyone from 10th pass onwards. No college degree needed.

  • Special Features: Free courses with certification. Learn in Hindi or local language. Courses useful for freelancing and jobs.

IIRS (Indian Institute of Remote Sensing – ISRO)

Website : https://www.iirs.gov.in/Edusat-News

  • What is it? Online learning by ISRO for students interested in space and science.

  • Courses Available: Satellite tech, GIS, remote sensing, Space applications in agriculture, water, urban planning, Disaster management using satellites.

  • Duration: 1 week to 4 weeks

  • Eligibility: Class 10+ students interested in space science. College students from science/tech field preferred.

  • Special Features: Courses by ISRO scientists. Online with live classes and materials. Free certificate after assignment completion.
